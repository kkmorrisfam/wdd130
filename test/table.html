EVERYBODY … with a sense of adventure can go river rafting in California. Using our knowledge of the rapids, we tailor our trips for all skill levels. Beginners will love our white water river rafting adventures on the South Fork Smith river, while those with a hankering for bigger thrills might go for our Middle Fork Smith river. We also offer white water rafting for kids as young as 4 on the South Fork Smith. Enjoy the beautiful scenic backdrop of the river as you bond with your fellow rafters and discover what makes white water rafting in California so special.
