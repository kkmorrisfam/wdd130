Overview

Purpose

We provide information to advertise and to book white water rafting trips.

Audience

We provide white water rafting trips from beginners to experienced adventurers. Do you work hard at your job and need a break? What are you doing this summer? Are you looking for a week away from the stresses of life and everyday work? Come and book a white water rafting trip with us. We have a raft waiting for you. Want to share this experience with your family or friends? We can book small parties or large parties.

Who are the target customers? Professionals and their families

What do they want? A weekend adventure away from the stresses of life and everyday work.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Access information on tours; where and how to book a trip; what ages and level of trips are booked (beginner, intermediate, experienced)?
