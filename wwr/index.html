THE RIVERS WE RUN

Our vision is to create a sustainable and equitable reality where our rivers are enjoyed and protected, a place where families and friends can be inspired and challenged.

White Water Rafting Company has been running rivers since we were first founded in 1985. While some things have changed over the years, we remain a family-run small business with the same passion for the river and the mission of leading others to experience it.
