Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the east, Wyoming ot the northeast, Idaho to the north, Arizona to the south, and Nevada to the west. it also touches the corner of New Mexico in the southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education information technology, and research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recrecation. Salt Lake Cityi, the state capital and largest city, is home to the headquarters The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints (LDS Church).
